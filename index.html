The flow

The flow

10 days of stories, secrets and moments that matter

10 days of stories, secrets and moments that matter

Day 1 : First Tastes of Hanoi

Start your journey by meeting your fellow tribe members

Experience Cha Ca La Vong's time-honoured tradition - turmeric-marinated fish with fresh dill (vegan options in another hidden gem)

Mount vintage USSR Minsk motorbikes, weaving through colonial villas and backstreets on a 5-hour cultural ride where 7-9 local specialities await tasting

Cross the historic Long Bien Bridge, discovering an off-grid island

End the night with a local beer crawl, where Hanoi's evening pulse comes alive

Day 2 : Stone giants of Ninh Binh

Drive to the ancient capital of Hoa Lu, where Dinh and Le Dynasty temples hold court

Climb centuries-old steps to Bich Dong Pagoda, where bells from 1428 still echo through time

Glide along Ngo Dong River in a sampan, where limestone karsts pierce emerald waters

Return to Hanoi as sunset paints the sky, ending with a traditional Vietnamese feast

Day 3 : Dawn rituals & coffee rituals

Join the 6 AM Tai Chi masters in their daily practice as fruit sellers arrange their morning wares by the Hoan Kiem Lake

Wander the Old Quarter's awakening streets, sampling dawn delicacies from pho to spring rolls

Transform into coffee artisans during our afternoon Barista Battle, crafting the perfect egg coffee

End your evening at a legendary restaurant, where traditional recipes tell stories of Vietnamese homes

Day 4: Mountain tales of Sapa

Journey five hours through changing landscapes, from delta plains to mountain peaks

Trek deep into the Hmong village of Lao Chai and Ta Van

Share afternoon tea with Black Hmong artisans, learning their centuries-old batik craft

Create your own batik piece, each pattern telling its own story

Day 5: Tribal wisdom of Sapa

Begin at Sapa's Gothic Stone Church, heart of the old French hill station

Marvel at Silver Waterfall's 200-meter cascade against mountain backdrops

Visit Ta Phin village, where Red Dao women share their ancestral spa wisdom

Unwind in a mountain-view herbal bath, prepared using age-old Red Dao recipes

Gather for a mountain sturgeon hotpot, where Sapa's flavours come together (vegan options available)

Day 6: Exploring ancient streets

Bid farewell to mountain mists and fly to Hoi An's coastal embrace

Cross Hoi An's 400-year-old Japanese Bridge as golden hour approaches

Float on a lantern-lit boat, releasing wishes into the gentle current

Play Street Food Bingo through ancient alleys, where each bite tells a story

Day 7: Culinary tales

Transform market shopping into a photo scavenger hunt through morning stalls

Join our Masterchef Challenge, crafting Tam Huu & Banh Xeo with local chefs

Experience Teh Dar Show's tribal rhythms, where UNESCO-heritage instruments sing ancient songs

Dine at local streets of Hoi An.

Day 8: Clouds and ghosts

Ascend Ba Na Hills by cable car, watching clouds part around the Golden Bridge

Journey to Hue via the serpentine road, where mountains embrace the sea

Discover the City of Ghosts, where $50,000 tombs tell tales of eternal love

End your day with Hue's street food specialities, from thick noodles to crispy pancakes

Day 9: Imperial waters

Meet the fishing families of Tam Giang Lagoon, living as their ancestors did

Learn net-casting techniques passed down through generations

Explore Hue's Imperial City, where Nguyen Dynasty spirits still whisper

Float down the Perfume River for a royal farewell dinner, dining as emperors once did

Day 10: Farewell dawn

Share a final morning with Hue's gentle awakening

Bid farewell to Vietnam, carrying stories that will last lifetimes

End of journey, but not of stories
